The Soul of a Supercar in the Body of an SUV.

The Lamborghini Urus isn't just an SUV; it is a declaration of dominance. Merging the soul of the Aventador with the versatility of a daily driver, the Urus stands as the world's first Super Sport Utility Vehicle. Powered by a thunderous 4.0-liter V8 twin-turbo engine producing 650 HP, every acceleration is a visceral experience that defies the laws of physics for a vehicle of its stature.

Step inside to find an aeronautics-inspired cockpit. The interior is a masterpiece of Italian craftsmanship, swathed in premium Alcantara and hand-stitched leather. The "Tamburo" driving mode selector allows you to transform the vehicle's character from the refined "Strada" for business arrivals to the aggressive "Corsa" for spirited coastal drives.

Whether you are making a grand entrance at a high-profile luxury event or navigating a weekend getaway through winding mountain roads, the Urus provides a dual personality of unparalleled luxury and untamed performance. It is the ultimate choice for those who refuse to compromise on either utility or prestige.
